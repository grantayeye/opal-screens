Proprietary Technology

BLACKFIRE

Engineered to Disappear

We built BlackFire around a single obsession: make the screen vanish so only the image remains. Our proprietary chip-on-board architecture fuses every pixel directly into the surface — no mounted LEDs, no visible grid. Just a seamless, light-absorbing canvas.

The anti-reflective surface eliminates glare even in sun-drenched rooms. The true-black pixel control means when a pixel is off, it's off — indistinguishable from the wall behind it. This is the technology we engineered for spaces where "good enough" was never part of the conversation.
