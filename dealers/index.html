Why Buy Through an Authorized Dealer?
An Opal Screens authorized dealer doesn't just sell you a display — they design, install, and support a complete solution tailored to your space.

Every project starts with a professional site survey and consultation. Your dealer will recommend the right series, pixel pitch, and size based on your room dimensions, viewing distances, and content needs.
