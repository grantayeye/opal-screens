# Opal Screens Blog Content Guide

Read this BEFORE writing any blog content. These are the facts.

## Product Series

### Onyx Series — Ultra-Fine Pitch
- **Pixel Pitch:** 0.7mm
- **Technology:** BlackFire (COB) + NanoPix (color accuracy)
- **Brightness:** 600 nits
- **Refresh Rate:** 60Hz
- **Color Depth:** 21-bit+
- **IP Rating:** IP43 (indoor)
- **Positioning:** Premium residential. Private home theaters, dedicated media rooms. Museum-quality detail at any viewing distance. The "no compromise" option.

### Boulder Series — Motion King
- **Pixel Pitch:** 0.9mm
- **Technology:** BlackFire (COB) + SilkStream (240Hz motion)
- **Brightness:** 1,000 nits
- **Refresh Rate:** 240Hz
- **Color Depth:** 21-bit+
- **IP Rating:** IP43 (indoor)
- **Positioning:** High-end residential sweet spot. Sports, cinema, gaming — anywhere motion matters. Best balance of detail and performance for most home theaters.

### Crystal Series — Premium Versatility
- **Pixel Pitch:** 0.9mm and 1.2mm options
- **Technology:** BlackFire (COB)
- **Brightness:** 600 nits
- **Refresh Rate:** 60Hz
- **Color Depth:** 21-bit+
- **IP Rating:** IP43 (indoor)
- **Positioning:** Premium residential. Living rooms, great rooms, larger viewing spaces. The 0.9mm matches Onyx-level clarity; 1.2mm is ideal for 4+ foot viewing distances.

### Water Series — Outdoor
- **Technology:** Outdoor-rated MicroLED
- **Color Depth:** 10-bit
- **IP Rating:** IP65 (weatherproof)
- **Positioning:** Outdoor entertainment — pools, patios, rooftop lounges. Built to handle sun, rain, and humidity.

### Doublet Series — Commercial Value
- **Pixel Pitch:** 1.5–2.5mm range
- **Color Depth:** 10-bit
- **Positioning:** Commercial environments — corporate lobbies, conference rooms, retail signage, digital menu boards, house of worship. Longer viewing distances make these pitches ideal. Cost-effective for large-format commercial installs.

## Core Technologies

### BlackFire (COB — Chip on Board)
- Opal's proprietary COB encapsulation. LEDs embedded directly in the substrate.
- Benefits: No exposed diodes, true blacks, extreme contrast (1,000,000:1+), impact resistant, no dead pixels from bumps.
- Present in: ALL series

### NanoPix
- Sub-pixel color calibration for perfect color accuracy across the entire wall.
- Benefits: No color banding, no tile-to-tile variation, gallery-grade uniformity.
- Present in: Onyx, Crystal

### SilkStream
- 240Hz native refresh rate for ultra-smooth motion.
- Benefits: Eliminates motion blur, judder, and tearing. Sports look live. Cinema looks filmic.
- Present in: Boulder ONLY
- ⚠️ **CRITICAL:** SilkStream does NOT exist at 0.7mm pixel pitch. Never combine SilkStream with Onyx. The technology isn't there yet.

## Brand Voice
- Professional but conversational — expert friend, not salesperson
- Confident without being arrogant
- Technical accuracy matters — don't guess specs, reference this doc
- Sprinkle product mentions naturally (2-3 per article max), never force them
- Never mention specific dollar prices (dealer model)
- Opal is a dealer/integrator brand — customers buy through authorized partners

## SEO Notes
- Always include proper meta title, description, og:tags
- Use BlogPosting JSON-LD structured data
- Target 800-1000 words per article
- Use h2 subheadings for scannability
- End with CTA section linking to https://opalscreens.com/#contact

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Saying Crystal is "commercial" — it's premium residential
- Saying Doublet is residential — it's commercial/value
- Combining SilkStream with 0.7mm pitch — doesn't exist
- Mentioning prices or MSRP — dealer model, no public pricing
- Being too salesy — inform first, brand second
