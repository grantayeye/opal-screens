Why MicroLED

The End of Compromise in Home Cinema

Projectors wash out in ambient light. Giant TVs have bezels and size limits. MicroLED video walls eliminate every compromise — delivering a seamless, self-lit display surface with true black levels, infinite contrast, and zero maintenance.

  • Self-emissive — no bulbs to replace, no fan noise, no warm-up time
  • True black levels with infinite contrast ratios via BlackFire technology
  • Consistent brightness and color accuracy for 100,000+ hours
  • Works beautifully in any lighting condition — lights on or off
  • Zero input lag for gaming and interactive content
  • Seamless — no bezels, no visible panels, one continuous image

BlackFire Technology

Every Opal Screens home theater panel features BlackFire — our proprietary chip-on-board architecture that fuses pixels directly into the surface. No exposed LEDs, no visible grid, no glare. Just a smooth, light-absorbing canvas where inactive pixels disappear completely into the wall.

Pixel Pitch Guide

Sitting 4–8 feet away? Go with 0.7mm (Onyx) for invisible pixels at arm's length.

Sitting 6–12 feet away? 0.9mm (Boulder or Crystal) is the sweet spot for most home theaters.

Sitting 10+ feet away? 1.2mm (Crystal) delivers excellent quality at a compelling value.

Common Questions
Home Theater FAQ

Opal Screens offers standard 16:9 sizes from 110 to 270 inches, plus fully custom dimensions. For a typical dedicated home theater with seating 8–14 feet from the screen, a 137-inch to 165-inch display is the most popular choice. Larger rooms or immersive setups often go 220 inches or beyond.

It depends on your priorities. The Onyx Series (0.7mm) delivers the finest detail for close viewing distances under 8 feet. The Boulder Series (0.9mm, 240Hz) is ideal for sports and action with zero motion blur. The Crystal Series (0.9mm or 1.2mm) offers exceptional all-around picture quality at a range of price points.

MicroLED walls outperform projectors in every lighting condition. They produce their own light, deliver true black levels with infinite contrast, require no bulb replacements, run silently, and maintain consistent brightness for 100,000+ hours. They also work beautifully with ambient light on — something projectors struggle with.

Opal Screens video walls are installed by authorized dealers — professional AV integrators trained on our products. Installation is clean and precise, with modular panels mounted to a wall structure. Once installed, there's virtually zero maintenance — no bulbs, no filters, no calibration drift. BlackFire's sealed surface resists dust and degradation.
