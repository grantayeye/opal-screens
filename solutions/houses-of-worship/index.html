Why MicroLED for Worship
A Display Worthy of Your Mission
From intimate chapels to multi-thousand-seat sanctuaries, Opal Screens video walls deliver a visual experience that serves your congregation and enhances every moment of worship.

Crystal-Clear Lyrics & Graphics

White text on black backgrounds achieves infinite contrast with BlackFire technology. Every word is sharp, legible, and distraction-free — no glowing grey backgrounds or washed-out text.

IMAG & Live Camera Feeds

Display live camera feeds of speakers and worship leaders so every congregant feels connected, no matter where they sit. Seamless multi-input support makes IMAG integration effortless.

No Projector Limitations

No bulb replacements, no dimming over time, no washout from stage lighting. MicroLED walls produce their own light and maintain consistent brightness and color for 100,000+ hours.

Silent Operation

Zero fan noise. During quiet moments of prayer, reflection, or acoustic worship, your display is completely silent — unlike projectors with audible cooling fans.

Works in Any Lighting

Stage lights, natural sunlight through windows, dim candlelight — your video wall delivers vibrant, clear content in every lighting condition your worship environment presents.

Zero Maintenance

Install it and forget about it. No bulbs, no filters, no calibration appointments. BlackFire's sealed surface resists dust, and the display maintains its quality year after year.

Worship Content

Designed for How Churches Actually Use Displays

We understand that church displays serve a unique purpose. They need to handle rapid transitions between lyrics, sermon slides, video playback, live camera feeds, and announcement loops — often in quick succession during a single service.

  • Worship lyrics with infinite contrast on true black backgrounds
  • Sermon graphics, Scripture, and presentation slides
  • Live camera (IMAG) for speaker magnification
  • Pre-service and post-service announcement loops
  • Video playback for sermon illustrations and worship media
  • Multi-window layouts — lyrics on one side, camera feed on the other

Custom Sizes for Your Stage

Every sanctuary is different. Opal Screens video walls are modular — we build to your exact dimensions. Standard 16:9 in 110" to 270", ultra-wide cinematic formats, side-by-side dual displays, or wrap-around stage configurations.

Your authorized dealer will survey your space and recommend the optimal size, placement, and series for your sanctuary's sight lines and seating layout.
