Commercial Advantages

Why Businesses Choose Opal Screens

Commercial environments demand displays that run 12–18 hours a day without compromise. Opal Screens video walls are engineered for exactly that — delivering consistent, maintenance-free performance year after year.

  • 100,000+ hour lifespan with no bulb or component replacements
  • Zero maintenance — sealed BlackFire surface resists dust and degradation
  • Multiple simultaneous inputs for multi-source content
  • Custom sizes and aspect ratios for any architectural space
  • Silent operation — no fans, no noise in quiet environments
  • Nationwide dealer network for professional installation and support

Total Cost of Ownership

Unlike projectors (bulb replacements, recalibration) or LCD video walls (bezel degradation, uneven aging), MicroLED video walls have virtually zero ongoing costs. No consumables, no maintenance visits, no color drift. The upfront investment delivers decades of reliable, stunning performance.
