Why MicroLED?
MicroLED is the first display technology built without compromise. Every wall is custom-sized to your space, with no bezels and no borders — the image runs edge to edge. Unlike OLED, there is zero burn-in risk, even with static content displayed around the clock. And with a 100,000+ hour rated lifespan, a MicroLED wall installed today will deliver the same performance decades from now.
